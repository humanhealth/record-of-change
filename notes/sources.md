# Sources

Every Google initiative named in the brief, with the source used. Verified against
official Google / Android / DeepMind pages in June 2026 unless marked otherwise. Do not
add a claim to the page without a source here.

## Confirmed

1. **Google Research, Health — embedded research model and focus areas**
   Research is run embedded with clinical, product and engineering teams; focus areas
   include imaging and diagnostics, genomics, mobile sensing and public health.
   - https://research.google/teams/health/
   - https://research.google/blog/google-research-2022-beyond-health/

2. **MedGemma — open models for medical text and image comprehension**
   Built on Gemma 3. Supports medical image interpretation, medical text comprehension
   and clinical reasoning. Google states it is not intended to be used without
   appropriate validation and adaptation for a specific use case.
   - https://research.google/blog/medgemma-our-most-capable-open-models-for-health-ai-development/
   - https://developers.google.com/health-ai-developer-foundations/medgemma/model-card

3. **AMIE — conversational diagnostic AI, real-world feasibility study**
   Prospective clinical feasibility study at Healthcare Associates, part of Beth Israel
   Deaconess Medical Center (BIDMC), Boston (Apr–Nov 2025; published 11 Mar 2026). A human
   AI supervisor was on hand against predefined safety criteria; no safety stop was
   triggered. Reported on par with primary care physicians on management plan and
   differential-diagnosis quality.
   - https://research.google/blog/exploring-the-feasibility-of-conversational-diagnostic-ai-in-a-real-world-clinical-study/
   - https://research.google/blog/advancing-amie-towards-specialist-care-and-real-world-validation/

4. **MedASR — medical speech to text**
   Announced alongside MedGemma 1.5.
   - https://research.google/blog/next-generation-medical-image-interpretation-with-medgemma-15-and-medical-speech-to-text-with-medasr/

6. **AlphaFold — protein-structure prediction / science discovery**
   - https://deepmind.google/science/alphafold/

7. **Android Health Connect — Medical Records (FHIR)**
   Health Connect stores and structures health and fitness data. Its Medical Records
   feature extends this to basic medical data (allergies, medications, immunisations, lab
   results) in FHIR format, with explicit user permission. Introduced with Android 16.
   - https://developer.android.com/health-and-fitness/health-connect/medical-records
